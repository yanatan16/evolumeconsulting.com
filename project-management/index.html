PROJECT MANAGEMENT IMPROVEMENTS

Project management can take many different forms depending on the a company’s size, product, and team structure. From quick action item reviews to weekly meetings to detailed daily reports and status tracking, EvoLume Consulting helps you determine the best project management solutions for your team. 

We have experience developing a wide variety of project management systems. The complexity of these systems match their intended purposes. For quick, simple projects, a collaborative task tracking spreadsheet or interactive app can meet the needs of a team. For multi-discipline, multi-project teams, more robust systems that utilize multi-platform apps or management software might be the key. Whatever your business’ needs, at EvoLume we know how to develop a road map to getting your projects on track. Scalable program management that grows with your company and provides ever increasing project efficiency and capabilities is our expertise.

We also pride ourselves on knowing the latest productivity apps and agile management strategies. With our research and review of these products, we can suggest the latest and greatest tools to keep your team communication, project scheduling, and requirements development up-to-date and ahead of the competition. We want you and your team to focus on creating the best product for your customers and let us take care of the details of implementing project management systems.

If you are looking for development of project management systems and program management services please contact EvoLume’s founder, Savannah Eisen, at seisen@evolumeconsulting.com.
